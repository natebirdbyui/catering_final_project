About HIPOC

Meet our owner and founder, Nathan Bird, who is passionate about providing exceptional catering services for all occasions. With years of experience in the culinary industry, Nathan has built HIPOC into a premier catering service known for its delicious cuisine and outstanding service. He has been recognized for his dedication to quality and customer satisfaction, and he takes pride in creating memorable dining experiences for every event.

He loves spending time with his family, exploring new culinary trends, and experimenting with innovative recipes to delight his clients. Nathan has a passion for cooking! He has enjoyed cooking since he was a child, and it has always been a big part of his life. He has loved cooking, grilling, smoking, and baking! He even incorporates his food that he loved when he served his church mission North of Mexico City! He has been told that his food is the closest to authentic Mexican food that they have had outside of Mexico. His friends and family loves when he cooks for them. They always know Nathan always deliver and they are never disappointed. He is always looking for new ways to improve his craft and provide the best possible catering experience for his clients.

Our mission at HIPOC is to provide exceptional catering services for all occasions. Whether you're planning a corporate event, wedding, or private party, HIPOC has you covered with outstanding service and delectable cuisine.
